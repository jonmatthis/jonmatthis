# FreeMoCap Curriculum

Welcome to the official curriculum for learning FreeMoCap - the Free Motion Capture project.

```{note}
This curriculum is designed to take you from complete beginner to specialized practitioner in motion capture technology, with tracks for Technology, Science, and Art applications.
```
