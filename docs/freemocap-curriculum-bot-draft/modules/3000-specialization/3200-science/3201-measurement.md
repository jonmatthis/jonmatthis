# 3201 - Intro to Measurement

```{note}
This module introduces fundamental concepts of measurement in motion capture research.
```

## Learning Objectives

- Understand measurement principles in motion capture
- Learn about accuracy, precision, and reliability
- Understand sources of measurement error
- Apply measurement concepts to motion capture data

## Topics Covered

- Measurement fundamentals
- Accuracy vs. precision
- Reliability and validity
- Sources of error in markerless motion capture
- Calibration and measurement quality
- Units and coordinate systems
