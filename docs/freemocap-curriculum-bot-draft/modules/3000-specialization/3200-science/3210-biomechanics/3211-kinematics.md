# 3211 - Kinematics, Joint Angles, and Center of Mass

```{note}
This module covers kinematic analysis, joint angle calculation, and center of mass estimation.
```

## Learning Objectives

- Understand kinematic vs. kinetic analysis
- Calculate joint angles from 3D coordinates
- Estimate whole-body center of mass
- Interpret kinematic data

## Topics Covered

- Kinematics fundamentals
- Position, velocity, acceleration
- Joint angle calculation methods
- Coordinate systems for joint angles
- Center of mass estimation
- Segment mass parameters
