## Choosing Your Path

Consider these questions when choosing your track:

- What drew you to motion capture in the first place?
- What do you want to create or accomplish?
- What skills do you want to develop?

```{tip}
You can explore multiple tracks! The tracks share foundational knowledge, so switching or combining paths is encouraged.
```
